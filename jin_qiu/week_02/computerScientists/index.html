Yukihiro Matsumoto

Yukihiro “Matz” Matsumoto is the the Chief Designer of the Ruby programming language in 1993 and released it to the public in 1995. Since then, Ruby and the framework Ruby on Rails have taken off, earning a seat at the programming table along side other popular languages like Java and PHP. Matz was recently hired by Heroku, the Ruby platform-as-a-service owned by Salesforce.com, as the chief architect of Ruby. Follow on Twitter
